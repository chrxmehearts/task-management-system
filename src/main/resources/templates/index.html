Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

A focused set of tools that help you stay organized without the bloat of heavyweight project management software.

Quick Task Creation

Create tasks in seconds. Add a title, description, priority, and due date — then get back to work immediately.

Priority Levels

Mark tasks as Low, Medium, or High priority. Color-coded cards make it instantly clear what deserves your attention first.

Status Tracking

Move tasks through Todo, In Progress, and Done. Filter your board to see exactly what you need, when you need it.

Due Date Tracking

Set deadlines on tasks and always know what's coming up. Never miss an important deadline or lose track of a deliverable.

Clean Dashboard

A distraction-free workspace that surfaces your tasks in a scannable card grid. Your stats — total, in progress, done — always at a glance.

Private & Secure

Your workspace is yours alone. JWT-secured authentication ensures only you can access your tasks and data.
